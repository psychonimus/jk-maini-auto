Raymond Group

A Legacy Woven with Excellence

One of India’s most respected names in branded fabric and fashion retail. Incorporated in 1925, Raymond has built a legacy of excellence in textile manufacturing, with an impressive capacity to produce 31 million meters of wool and wool-blended fabrics.

The Group owns a distinguished portfolio of apparel brands including Raymond, Premium Apparel, Raymond Made to Measure, Ethnix, Park Avenue Woman, ColorPlus, Kamasutra, and Parx. These brands are available through our exclusive retail chain, The Raymond Shop (TRS), which has a strong presence with over 700 stores across more than 200 cities in India and overseas.

As a group, Raymond is involved in numerous businesses and has a portfolio of well-known brands in Textiles, Apparel, Denim, Consumer Care, Engineering and Real Estate.

About JK Maini Group

United by Excellence. Driven by Precision.

JK Maini is the new powerhouse in precision manufacturing formed by the strategic merger of three industry leaders:

Raymond Engineering (now known as J K Maini Precision Technology) is the convergence of three industry powerhouses Maini Precision Products Ltd., Ring Plus Aqua Ltd., and JK Files Ltd. each with a deep-rooted legacy in precision manufacturing, engineering excellence, and global quality standards.

This strategic integration brings together decades of specialized expertise, world-class manufacturing capabilities, and innovation-driven cultures to form one unified entity with a bold vision to engineer the future of mobility, infrastructure, and industrial growth.

Backed by the trust and legacy of the Raymond Group, this new engineering powerhouse is primed to deliver advanced solutions across automotive, aerospace, industrial, and tooling sectors. With cutting-edge infrastructure, future-ready technologies, and a relentless focus on quality, Raymond Engineering is not just keeping pace with change it is shaping it.

Together, we bring decades of expertise in precision components, motion systems, and industrial tools, now aligned under one unified vision J K Maini Precision Technology.

As a proud part of the Raymond Group legacy, J K Maini Precision Technology is built on the foundation of craftsmanship, innovation, and quality. Our integrated capabilities now span across passenger and commercial vehicle components, industrial tooling, powertrain systems, and more.

Three Legacies. One Future. Welcome to J K Maini Precision Technology.
