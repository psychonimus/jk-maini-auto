Off Highway & Industrial Applications

Engineered to Strengthen Every Industrial Process

From rugged construction equipment and forestry machinery to marine systems, power gensets, and automated industrial setups—today’s off-highway and industrial applications demand uncompromising reliability, precision, and durability.
These environments operate under extreme conditions—high pressure, fluctuating loads, and harsh terrains—where performance and safety are non-negotiable.
At the core of this performance lies precision manufacturing, enabling flawless production of mission-critical components like input shafts, valve blocks, and cylinder assemblies with micron-level tolerances and high-quality surface finishes.
Whether it's sustaining marine operations, optimizing energy systems, mechanizing forestry operations, or supporting remote construction projects, precision engineering ensures longer product life cycles, reduced downtime, and consistent system efficiency.
As industries evolve, so must the components that drive them. And we're delivering just that—with performance built to endure, across every application.

Our Expertise

At JK Maini Precision Products, we specialize in delivering high-precision components tailored for the demanding needs of the industrial and hydraulics sector. Our core product portfolio includes bearing housings, pressure plates, wheel forks, brake plates, ELSD housings, bearing journals, cradles, and door assembly child parts, along with precision-engineered power tool parts, drill bits, and reamers.

We work with a range of high-grade materials including alloy steels, stainless steels, and aluminum alloys, capable of withstanding high-pressure and high-stress environments. Our manufacturing processes support tight tolerances (as low as ±5 microns) and are optimized for both low-volume prototyping and high-volume serial production.

Industry Standards and Compliance

We ensure full compliance with globally recognized quality and process standards such as IATF 16949 and ISO 9001, with a strong emphasis on process validation, traceability, and zero-defect delivery. Our capabilities make us a trusted partner for OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers in the industrial and hydraulics space worldwide.
