Aerospace & Defence Sector

Propelling Aerospace Innovation with Precision and Performance

JK Maini is a trusted partner in the aerospace and defense sectors, delivering critical components that meet the highest standards of precision and reliability. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are certified to AS9100D and NADCAP standards, ensuring compliance with the most stringent aerospace quality requirements.

With decades of expertise in precision machining, JK Maini has established itself as a premier supplier of complex aerospace components. Our capabilities span from structural assemblies to critical engine parts, supported by advanced CNC machining centers and comprehensive quality control systems.

Our commitment to aerospace excellence is demonstrated through our extensive portfolio of certified components, serving major aircraft manufacturers and defense contractors worldwide. Each component undergoes rigorous testing and validation to ensure it meets the exacting standards of the aerospace industry.
