Recognized for Agility in Crisis

Skoda Auto Commendation for Supply Chain Excellence

In a testament to our commitment to operational resilience and global supply chain excellence, J K Maini (formerly Ring Plus Aqua Ltd.) was recognized by SKODA Auto Volkswagen India for swiftly stepping in during a critical supply disruption.

In August, catastrophic floods in Slovenia halted production at a key European supplier of ring gears for dual-mass flywheels—essential components in internal combustion engine (ICE) vehicles. Faced with a potential production bottleneck impacting brands like Audi, ŠKODA, Porsche, and Volkswagen, the procurement teams at ŠKODA Auto turned to trusted Indian partners.
