Automobile

At JK Maini, we specialize in precision manufacturing of high-quality automobile parts that power the vehicles of today and tomorrow. Our advanced engineering processes and rigorous quality control ensure every component meets the demanding standards of the automotive industry.

JK Maini

Driving Precision and Innovation in the Global Auto Components Industry

JK Maini is a leading player in the global auto components industry, holding a dominant market share of over 50% in the domestic passenger vehicle (PV) segment and more than 45% in the domestic commercial vehicle (CV) segment for supplies to original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) during Fiscal 2021.

With a global reputation in the ring gears industry, JK Maini has built its strength on a foundation of tailor-made engineering solutions and long-standing partnerships with OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers. This strategic edge has enabled the company to expand its portfolio to include high-precision flexplates and water pump bearings, reinforcing its position as a full-spectrum auto component manufacturer.

As the only domestic manufacturer of flexplates in India, JK Maini fulfilled over 25% of the domestic flexplate demand for passenger vehicles in Fiscal 2021 — a testament to its unmatched manufacturing capabilities and industry leadership.

Automotive and Industrial Applications

With an inventory of over 750 stock keeping units (SKUs) across ring gears, flexplates, and water pump bearings, JK Maini caters to a wide range of automotive and industrial applications, including:

Construction and mining equipment
Agricultural tractors
Industrial power generators
Lawn and garden equipment

We power the future of mobility with high-precision components engineered for excellence across all automotive segments. Our precision manufacturing capabilities support OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers globally with components built for durability, efficiency, and next-gen mobility. With segment-specific expertise and a deep understanding of drivetrain, powertrain, and e-mobility systems, we help move the world—one component at a time.
