Electric & Hybrid Sector

Powering the Future of Electric & Hybrid Mobility

The Electric and Hybrid vehicle industry is driving the future of sustainable mobility, demanding rapid innovation, compact design, and uncompromising efficiency. As powertrains evolve from combustion to electric, manufacturers face challenges like thermal management, lightweighting, and tight tolerance requirements for high-speed rotating parts. Precision manufacturing plays a pivotal role in overcoming these challenges—ensuring components like motor shafts, hub covers, and transmission parts meet exact specifications for performance, durability, and safety. In this high-growth, high-expectation sector, precision isn't just a requirement—it's the key to unlocking reliability and range.

Our Expertise in Electric & Hybrid Mobility

Core Components & Systems

We support the next wave of clean mobility with high-precision components tailored for electric and hybrid vehicles. Our product portfolio includes:

  • Powertrain & Transmission: Transmission systems, input shafts, clutch flanges
  • Motor & Drive Assemblies: BLDC hub covers, motor shafts, spiders
  • Functional Parts: Park pawls, lever arms, oil sleeves, support brackets, sand covers

Materials, Tolerances & Volume

We support the next wave of clean mobility with high-precision components tailored for electric and hybrid vehicles. Our product portfolio includes:

  • Materials: Transmission systems, input shafts, clutch flanges
  • Tolerances: Precision-machined to sub-micron levels, ensuring optimal motor performance and energy efficiency
  • Volume Capabilities: Scalable production—from prototypes to high-volume series manufacturing for OEMs and Tier 1s

Industry Standards & Compliance

We align our processes with the evolving benchmarks of e-mobility—ensuring thermal stability, magnetic balance, and tight concentricity for motor components. All parts are built to meet rigorous industry norms and are fully validated for integration into electric powertrains.
